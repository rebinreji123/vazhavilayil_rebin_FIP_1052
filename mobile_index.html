Services provided by our library.

Borrowing materials: Libraries offer books, magazines, CDs, DVDs, and other materials that users can borrow for a set period of time. Reference and research assistance: Librarians are available to help users find information on a wide range of topics and to provide research assistance.

Computer and internet access: Many libraries offer computer and internet access to their users, which is particularly important for those who do not have access to these resources at home. Programs and events: Libraries often host programs and events such as book clubs, author talks, storytimes, and workshops on a variety of topics. Interlibrary loan: Libraries can borrow materials from other libraries on behalf of their users, expanding the range of materials available to them.
